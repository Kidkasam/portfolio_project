About Me

I started coding a few years ago because I liked the idea of making something out of nothing. I mostly work with standard web technologies—I'm a big believer in not over-engineering things if you don't have to. Sometimes vanilla HTML and CSS is all you need.

When I'm not staring at a terminal or fighting with a CSS grid, you'll probably find me experimenting with new pour-over coffee techniques, hiking a local trail, or tweaking my dotfiles for the hundredth time. Always learning, always tinkering.
